About Us


The expRestaurant story begins in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia in the 1990’s. Evelyn Overton found a recipe in the local newspaper that would inspire her “Original” cheeseburger. Everyone loved her recipe so much that she decided to open a small Burger shop, but she eventually gave up her dream of owning her own business in order to raise her two small children, David and Renee. She moved her cooking equipment to a kitchen in her basement and continued to supply meals to several of the best restaurants in town while raising her family.

Our Vision
To serve happiness to our customers through delicious, quality meals and extraordinary restaurant experience while working toward the greater good for our employees, community and environment.

Our Mission
To enhance our employee’s excellence, continuous development and happiness
To generate a sustainable profit growth for our company’s shareholders
